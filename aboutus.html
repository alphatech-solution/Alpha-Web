About Us

Founded in 2016, Alpha Tech Solutions is a dynamic software training institute dedicated to empowering individuals with the skills and knowledge needed to excel in the IT industry. We offer a wide range of professional IT courses both online and offline, providing flexible training solutions to meet the diverse needs of our students. Whether you prefer the convenience of online learning or the interactive experience of in-person classes, Alpha Tech Solutions has you covered. Our comprehensive training programs cover the latest technologies and industry trends, equipping our students with the expertise they need to succeed in today's competitive job market. From beginner-level courses to advanced certifications, we offer personalized coaching, hands-on labs, and career placement assistance to support our students every step of the way.

What is Alpha Tech Solutions ?

Alpha Tech Solutions is an online transformative upskilling platform for working tech professionals. Our industry-vetted approach towards teaching & training young professionals not only helps them upskill but also #CreateImpact in the real world. We are devoted to creating an ecosystem that nurtures our learners and assists them in unlocking talent, skills & opportunities at every stage of their careers. Learners enrolled with us are taught, guided, and mentored by industry veterans and experts from leading tech organisations.

Mission
